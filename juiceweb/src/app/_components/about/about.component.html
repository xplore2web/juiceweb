About Us

Feel Fresh is a juice ordering and delivery company based in Bangalore, India. FeelFresh was inspired by the thought of providing a complete Juice ordering and delivery solution from the best neighbourhood juice centers. A single window for ordering from a wide range of Juice shops, we have our own exclusive fleet of delivery personnel to pickup orders from centers and deliver it to customers . Having our own fleet gives us the flexibility to offer customers a no minimum order policy on any shops and accept online payments for all partner centers that we work with. Our delivery personnel carry one order at a time which ensures we get reliable and fast deliveries."
